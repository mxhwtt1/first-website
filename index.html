Why does this matter?

💓

CR saves lives

People who complete cardiac rehab are significantly less likely to be re-admitted to hospital or to die from heart disease.

💪

Anyone can do it

You do not need to be fit, young, or healthy to start. CR is designed for people right after a heart event, at any age.

🏭

It is free on the NHS

Cardiac rehab costs you nothing. No gym memberships, no equipment, no hidden fees. Just ask for a referral.
