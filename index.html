🤸 PNF
Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation — Stretching technique using contract–relax muscle actions to increase flexibility and range of motion.
💪 PAP
Post-Activation Potentiation — Temporary performance boost after a heavy or explosive contraction due to increased neuromuscular activation.
⚡ EMS
Electrical Muscle Stimulation — Electrical impulses applied to muscles to cause contractions for training, recovery, or rehabilitation.
